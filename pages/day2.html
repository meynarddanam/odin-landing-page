How To Tokyo
"More than any other city, Tokyo demonstrates that 'city' is a verb, and not a noun" -Toshiko Mori
Ride The Train
Be a real Tokyoite. Parking is expensive and hard to find. Just take the train and walk.
And you will need to walk a lot... You know, if you want to...
Eat Lots Of Foods
Like, a lot.
If I had to eat only in one city for the rest of my life, Tokyo would be it.
-Anthony Bourdain
Eat MORE Foods
We really ate a lot.
From fried stuffs
to raw seafoods
to cool jelly
to hot curry, which was too hot.
Watch People
Take a selfie. Watch the couple behind you who may or may not be in the middle of a fight.
And Cultures
Of course we could not go to one of Japan's ancient capital and not visit the traditional spots; Tsurugaoka Hachimangu Shrine, Hokoku-ji Temple, Studio Ghibli store, and the railway crossing from Slam Dunk.
Sadly looking for a parking spot near the railway crossing was not that easy. And I needed to pee. And the traffic was moving very slowly. And we missed the sunset.
And That's How You Get Over Jet Lag
By taking a late night stroll to your hotel, slightly drunk.
